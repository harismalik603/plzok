This study group is initiated to help students from Batch 35 CNC group, who wants to join PIAIC Bootcamp 2020. The main motto of the group is to help each other in this journey, so one can become proficient enough to join Team Ninja of PanaCloud led by Sir Zia. Please be aware this is only a study group and NOT an official group of PIAIC. You can find details of BootCamp here:link
